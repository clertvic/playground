Interactive Choropleth Map

Hover over a state to get more information on the selected commodity in that state. You will see a line graph for that state in the timeline. Click on a state to see more details at county level.

4 Stats for Each Commodity

Use these controls to filter data by total area planted and harvested as well as total yield and production of the selected commodity.

Time Slider

Drag the time slider to see the data change over time on the map. You can click anywhere on the time slider to quickly move to a specific year.
